About Barbados

Every year many students from across the world get admission in top medical universities in Barbados to study MBBS in Barbados. Barbados is a beautiful small island country in the Lesser Antilles, in the Americas. It is situated in the western area of the North Atlantic and 100 km (62 mi) east of the Windward Islands and the Caribbean Sea.

The name 'Barbados' was derived from the Bearded Fig Trees which were found in abundance at some point of time. This island is completely surrounded by the Atlantic Ocean.

Barbados is a mix of culture, food, nature, the sea and amazing climate. The climate in Barbados from June to November is rainy and is called the "wet season". from December to May, the weather is dry and is called the "dry season".

Barbados is member of the World Trade Organization (WTO) and participates actively in its work. English is official language of this country which helps international students to adapt easily.The literacy rate is 100% in this country.

MBBS In Barbados


Barbados follows the US Education system and curriculum and hence is focused towards preparing Physician workforce for countries like USA, Canada, etc. The quality of education in this country is comparable to USA, UK and other developed countries. Medical students pursuing their MBBS degree from Barbados get all the facilities at a very low cost. Admission in most of the medical universities in Barbados does not require IELTS or TOEFL. Medical students studying in Barbados Medical colleges also get a chance to do clinical rotations program of 2 years in teaching hospitals in USA and UK which is a great opportunity for them.

The medical universities in Barbados are recognized and approved by the MCI - Medical Council of India; ECFMG – Education Council for Foreign Medical Graduates, USA; FAIMER’s IMED – Foundation for Advancement of International Medical Education and Research’s International Medical Education Directory; ACGME – Accreditation Council for Graduate Medical Education; MCC – Medical Council of Canada and WHO – World Health Organization



Benefits of MBBS from Barbados

  1. The MBBS degree from a Barbados medical University is recognized by WHO and UNESCO and is accepted globally.
  2. No entrance test is required for admission which makes the admission process much easier.
  3. Students are provided with the practicals and experiments to improve their skills in a better way.
  4. Students studying in medical colleges in Barbados are given a chance to get an internship in some of the best multinational companies and hospitals of the world.
  5. The quality of infrastructure, education and accommodation is comparable to other developed countries.
  6. The hospitals are well equipped with great infrastructure and technology.
  7. The medium of teaching is English which helps the medical students from around the world to learn better.
